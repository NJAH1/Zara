About Us

Who are we

We are an Omani partner company - founded business that operates with the customer focus and values we started with. Our partnership was established in 2007, since then our experience and expertise in the construction industry has developed to enable us to anticipate and respond quickly to our customers requirements.

Strive to exceed customers' expectations through competence, innovation, and teamwork.

Probatively explore policy and product opportunities to serve our customers better.

Aim to "do it right" the first time.

Vision

To provide exceptional construction services with high quality and affordable prices. We work to give our clients the chance of having innovative construction methodologies with Zero Harm.

Mission

▸ To provide the highest level of quality construction services at competitive prices.
▸ To establish customer satisfaction through quality, timeliness, attention to detail and service-minded attitudes.
▸ To maintain the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty, and fairness in our relationships with our suppliers, subcontractors, professional associates, and customers.

Our core values

Our Core Values drives us forward as an organization and bind us together as community, ZARA L.L.C has maintained its status and reputation by incorporating our values in our work.
